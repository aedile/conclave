## Operational Notes

### Known Issue: Parallel DP Training Budget Exhaustion

During this run, an attempt to run 4 jobs in parallel with `total_epochs=10` triggered
a `BudgetExhaustionError` from the per-job `allocated_epsilon=50.0` guard in
`DPCompatibleCTGAN`. This guard is independent from the global ledger and fires when
a single training run consumes more than 50 epsilon (which occurs for complex tables
like `customers` with high-cardinality `phone`/`address` columns under deep WGAN training).

**Impact**: Jobs 9–12 (earlier attempts) were left in TRAINING state because
`BudgetExhaustionError` from the training loop was not caught by `TrainingStep.execute()`.
`TrainingStep` only catches `RuntimeError`, but `BudgetExhaustionError` does not inherit from it.

**Workaround for this run**: Reduced epochs (`total_epochs=3`, `checkpoint_every_n=3`)
to ensure epsilon stays within the per-job 50.0 guard. All 4 Phase 37 jobs completed successfully.

**Pre-existing bug**: `BudgetExhaustionError` thrown during WGAN training loop bypasses
the job `FAILED` status update. This is a known gap in `TrainingStep` exception handling
(the step catches `RuntimeError` but not `BudgetExhaustionError`). The global ledger
was not deducted because budget exhaustion happened before the accounting step.
